Welcome to the 30th Annual Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival!

This year we are hosting a special listing of films to help celebrate this special occasion. We are going to show our original line up of Fantasy films that our festival first aired in 1988.

We will be showing three classical films, the first is The Princess Bride, Labyrinth and The Wizard of Oz.

Released in 1987
